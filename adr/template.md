# ADR NNNN: <short title of the decision>

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** <names>

## Context

What forces are at play — technical, product, domain? What makes this a decision worth
recording rather than an obvious default? State the problem, not the solution.

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly in active voice: "We will …". Be specific enough that someone
could implement it, or contest it, from this paragraph alone.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what we now have to live with. Include the
downsides honestly — an ADR that lists only benefits isn't a decision, it's an advert.

## Alternatives considered

The options we rejected and the one-line reason each lost. This is often the most useful
section a year later.
